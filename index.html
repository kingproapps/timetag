Industry-Leading Tool

Engineered for Accuracy and Simplicity

Timetag converts your mobile device into an unalterable auditing device. Perfect for construction managers, land surveyors, logistics drivers, insurance inspectors, and field engineers.

Precise Geotagging

Automatically log latitude, longitude, elevation, compass direction, and horizontal accuracy radius directly on your media. Includes UTM/MGRS grid formats.

Military-Grade Timing

Sync with atomic NTP servers to prevent manual device-time alterations. Supports timecodes up to milliseconds and local, UTC, or custom timezones.

Barcode & QR Scan Overlay

Instantly scan packages, items, asset tags, or QR links. The decoded value and format type is automatically imprinted onto the photo with geotagging metadata.

Unmatched Layout Control

Use custom brand fonts like Courier Prime. Color-code tags, define sizes, adjust positioning, and append customized project notes or logo watermarks.

100% Offline Integrity

No cellular connection? No problem. Timetag utilizes offline GPS triangulation models to cache high-accuracy location metadata and sync immediately when cell towers return.

Categorized Folders

Organize your scans and pictures into project-specific folders. Export complete sheets with embedded EXIF headers and tabular CSV exports for easy reporting.

Interactive Sandbox

Design Your Perfect Stamp

Experiment with styling before downloading. Switch layout templates, swap the underlying test media to simulate real-world environments, or choose your target stamp color.
